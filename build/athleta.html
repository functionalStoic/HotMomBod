How it Works

In the program I give you a basic overview of why our bodies store fat. Then I explain how you can find out how much energy (food) your body needs on a daily basis. I’ll teach you how to keep track of the food you’re eating so that you know how much fuel to give your body. You won’t be required to exclude any specific food groups. You’ll be encouraged to indulge in moderation. You’ll learn exactly how you can enjoy a life that can include bread, wine and chocolate and enjoy looking in the mirror.

Why it Works

Most busy moms just don't have time to cook every meal from scratch, spend two hours at the gym each day, keep the house clean and tidy, and be a good mommy. In fact, spending even an hour per day on ourselves sometimes feels selfish, if not impossible. Heck, just taking a shower feels like a victory. Hot Mom Bod works because it's SIMPLE and be done with even the busiest schedule. You focus on one thing: taking control of what you put in your mouth. Exercise is amazing but not everyone has time for it. On this program, it's not required and weight loss is guaranteed.
